I began travelling at a young age; in fact, I had a passport before I knew how to walk.
I have travelled to 16 countries and explored 32 of the 50 United States.
The lessons learned while travelling are not ones you can learn in the classroom,
the sites seen can't be replaced by panoramic film shots,
and no mattress in the world can replace the
satisfaction you feel the first night back home in your own bed.

My Travels

In today's fast-paced world, it is east to get caught up in a routine. Traveling can be perceived as the golden opportunity to "escape" from reality, and an opportunity to step into a new environment where you can learn a bit more about yourself. Life is unique and a mystery to be explored and for me, travel unleashes personal creativity, allows me to explore the unknown, connects me with those I care about the most, and provides and a thrill that is unparalleled to anything else I have ever experienced.
